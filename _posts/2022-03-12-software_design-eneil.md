## Communication is Key

To build on last week's accomplishments, the software team is wroking on implementing a bidirectional Bluetooth communication betweent the Arduino and the Unity platform, that serves the same functions as serial communication. It was discovered that Bluetooth Low Energy does not work on the Inwdows platform (which is needed for the Leap Motion-Unity development). As such, the team has switched to standard Bluetooth and is continuing development. Full steam ahead !!
